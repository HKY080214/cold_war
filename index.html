Rules

Victory: The game lasts 30 rounds. The higher final score wins.

Scoring: Each controlled region is worth 2 points. Each dominated region gives +1 extra. If you control more regions than your opponent, gain +2.

Control: A region is controlled if your influence is at least 6 and you lead by 4 or more.

Dominance: A region is dominated if your influence is at least 9 and you lead by 7 or more.

Round flow: Draw 1 card, then play 1 card. Turn order switches starting from Round 16.

Special: UN Veto is a restrictive card. It only triggers when the opponent plays an event card.
